Our Background

Who we are & What we do?

Timac Medical Solutions (Pty) Ltd currently offers a full range of both capital and disposable medical equipment. Established by a female who focuses on providing top-quality products and a world-class service nationwide.
We offer a wide selection of products, as well as specialist medical and surgical equipment. Our customers range from veterinarians, dentists, surgeries, pharmacies, general merchants and many other medical businesses in South Africa.

 

Our team strives to always provide a wider range, better prices and quicker, more efficient deliveries to our customers. After all, we understand the importance of quick and efficient deliveries - the faster our turnaround time, the better for your business!  This is also why you’ll find that our products are priced to give you substantial returns. Timac Medical Solutions (Pty) Ltd has three branches and customer support teams, two in Limpopo and Gauteng. We offer speedy deliveries and we service all areas in South Africa.
